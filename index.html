Non-Equilibrium Dynamics of SGD

In this work I explore the link between the Gibbs–Boltzmann distribution and the (non)-equilibrium state reached by SGD.
Workflow: SGD -> Langevin SGE -> Fokker-Planck -> Gibbs–Boltzmann.
